Who We Are

POGUNS Global Solutions Limited is a diversified Nigerian company delivering integrated solutions across aviation, marine, oil & gas, civil construction, logistics, real estate, trading, information technology, mining and corporate investment management consultancy services.

We operate as a single-point partner for consultancy, procurement, supply chain management, offshore services and general trading in complex and regulated industries.

Our commitment to integrity, operational excellence and regulatory compliance enables us to provide dependable solutions to both public and private sector clients.
